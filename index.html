The Grange
HOTEL


Where classic British heritage embraces modern luxuries with style, romance, chracter and vision. A Grade II former gentleman's residence, Kenstisbury Grange has been elegantly restored to its distinguished best. This fine Victorian country house hotel is an inspiring blend of glamour, history, nature and indulgence.


From the picture perfect setting in idyllic Devonshire countryside to the sublime, hand-made beds; from the genteel spirit of timeless English tradition to the impeccable attention to detail, Kentisbury Grange is the ultimate romantic getaway and luxury bolt-hole all rolled into one exquisite chic retreat where the best of old meets new.

In The
AREA


From its setting on the edge of Exmoor National Park, Kentisbury Grange commands some of the country's most arresting views of wide, sweeping horizons and panoramic vistas of seemingly endless natural beauty. In this unspoilt landscape, space and time seem infinite and it is impossible not to connect with nature.


The stunning beauty of Exmoor can be explored by foot on limitless picturesque walks, whilst cyclists can track the breathing coastline on the 30 mile Tarken Trail.Just a short drive away, the magnificent dune-lined beaches of Saunton Sands and Ilfracombe are crying out to be explored.
